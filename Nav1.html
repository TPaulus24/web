DTS GT200

Enjoy the flight more than build. The DTS GT200 is ready for race right after you open the box. Pilots need not to tune the quad but only install the receiver. The 4S power system comprises of 2206-2500Kv naked-bottom motors for agile response and 30A ESCs with BLHeli-S and Dshot 600 compatibility. An Omnibus F4 SD flight controller controls the quad comprehensively, when transmitting the user-configurable data to the on-screen display through the Betaflight OSD (Version 3.2.4). The Runcam Eagle 2 Pro GWY Edition camera with 2.1mm lens transmits distinctive and clear video through the GWY variable power video transmitter to a compatible 5.8GHz monitor or goggles. Furthermore, you can choose among 25, 200 or 600mW video transmitter power modes, you can even change your band and channel directly from your transmitter or on the main control unit built on the GT200. All these incredible components are implanted in this brand new impact-resistant main control unit which protects all essential electronics.
